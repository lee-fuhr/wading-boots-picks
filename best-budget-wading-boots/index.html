Felt-soled wading boots remain a go-to choice for fly anglers who fish slick, algae-covered rocks in rivers and streams. Rubber outsoles with felt provide superior grip on wet stone, making them essential for safe wading in many trout waters. On a budget, you don’t have to sacrifice traction or durability if you pick the right pair. When shopping for budget felt-soled boots, focus on fit, outsole quality, and construction. Look for boots that hold up after a season of gravel and sand, with comfortable footbeds and secure lacing.
